Sexual Assault Allegations



Here are the sexual assault allegations levelled against President Trump and Joe Biden:



President Donald Trump

Has been accused of rape, sexual assault,and sexual harassment including non-consensual kissing or groping.

  • So far 26 women have accused President Trump of sexual assault
  • These accusations have resulted in at least four lawsuits by Jill Harth (1997), 'The Apprentice' contestant Summer Zervos (2017), Alva Johnson (2019), and writer E. Jean Carroll (2019).
  • Most allegations against him came up after a 2005 Access Hollywood recording was leaked during the 2016 presidential campaign. In the recording, he was heard saying that a celebrity like himself "can do anything" to women, including "just start kissing them" and "grab 'em by the pussy."
  • He allegedly walked inside the changing rooms of models competing in the Miss USA and Miss Teen USA pageant as he owned both the competitions. He said in an interview that he could easily get away with these acts.
  • In May 2016, NYT published the article "Crossing the Line: How Donald Trump Behaved with Women in Private" in which several women detailed instances of the President's inappropriate behavior.
  • In 2019, the book 'All the President's Women: Donald Trump and the Making of a Predator', was published, which contained 43 additional allegations of sexual misconduct against Trump.
  • Trump has denied the allegations, saying he has been the victim of media bias, conspiracies, and a political smear campaign.
